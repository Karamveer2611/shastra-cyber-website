The Challenge

Why OT Cybersecurity Matters

Operational technology environments were designed for reliability, not security. As they connect to IT networks and the internet, they become vulnerable to attacks that can cause physical damage, operational downtime, and safety incidents.

📈

Rising Threat Landscape

Attacks on industrial systems have increased significantly. Nation-state actors and ransomware groups specifically target OT environments for maximum impact.

🔌

IT/OT Convergence Risk

As industrial systems connect to enterprise networks, attackers can move laterally from IT to OT — reaching critical control systems through traditional IT vulnerabilities.

⚠

Legacy System Vulnerabilities

Many industrial systems run for 20–30 years. They were never designed with cybersecurity in mind, creating vulnerabilities that cannot be patched in traditional ways.
